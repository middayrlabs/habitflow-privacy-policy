Privacy Policy for HabitFlow

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

At HabitFlow, your privacy is a core principle. This app is designed as an offline-first application, meaning your habit data never leaves your device.

Section 01 – Information We Do Not Collect

HabitFlow does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on external servers. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Account credentials or authentication data
  • Health, fitness, or behavioral data you enter into the app

HabitFlow does not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, or contact details. However, third-party services like Google AdMob may use device-level identifiers such as the Advertising ID.

HabitFlow itself does not collect or transmit usage analytics, crash reports, or telemetry to our own servers. However, third-party services such as Google AdMob may collect limited usage and interaction data for advertising purposes.

All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all data.

Section 02 – Third-Party Services (AdMob)

HabitFlow uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • Approximate location (country or region level)
  • Ad interaction data (such as impressions and clicks)

This data is considered non-personal and is used for advertising and performance purposes.

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy. HabitFlow does not control how this data is used.

Learn more: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Section 03 – Cookies and Tracking

HabitFlow does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

However, Google AdMob may use cookies or similar technologies to provide and measure advertisements.

Section 04 – Data Security

All data remains on your device and is protected by your device’s built-in security systems.

Section 05 – Children's Privacy

HabitFlow does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

Section 06 – Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use means acceptance of updates.
